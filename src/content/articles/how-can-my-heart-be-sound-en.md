## How Can My Heart Be Sound?

This article summarizes a short book devoted to the sound heart, presenting سلامة القلب as
one of the greatest aims of the believer's life. It treats the sound heart not as a vague
emotional state, but as a heart ordered rightly in its loyalties, loves, reactions, and
duties.

The article moves through several axes of that soundness: sincerity to Allah, submission
to His Messenger, sound regard for the Companions, healthy relation to scholars and people
of virtue, and purity of heart toward the general body of Muslims. In each case, the heart
is examined by what it affirms, rejects, and carries toward others.

It also emphasizes that a sound heart is inseparable from practical religion. Correct
belief, righteous conduct, mercy, counsel, and avoidance of harm are all presented as
fruits of inward soundness rather than external add-ons.

Its final impression is hopeful but demanding: a sound heart is a gift from Allah, but it
is sought through supplication, labor, reform, and disciplined attention to what fills the
inner life.
