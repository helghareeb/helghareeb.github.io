This summary introduces the book *How Can My Heart Be Sound?* by Prof. Dr. Ahmad Abdelrahman Al-Naqeeb. The article presents the sound heart as one of the greatest divine blessings and as a central spiritual aim from which righteous action follows.

It highlights the book's clear structure and notes several of its major themes: the soundness of the heart toward Allah, toward the Prophet's guidance, toward the Companions, and toward scholars and people of truth and virtue. The article also notes that the book originated as a scholarly lecture that was later transcribed and lightly revised into a more accessible written form.
