This companion notebook gathers the main creedal and faith-centered themes highlighted in the lessons.

## Main themes

- Divine attributes are affirmed as revelation affirms them, without distortion or anthropomorphic comparison.
- The preservation of revelation in this ummah is one of the great signs of the perfection of the religion.
- Legal judgment and prohibition belong to God alone; unauthorized legislation is a grave violation.
- Faith in God is inseparable from faith in His Messenger.
- Full faith requires inward submission to the Prophetic ruling without resistant inner resentment.
- Gradual legislation and abrogation reflect wisdom, mercy, and pedagogy.
- The ummah as a whole does not collectively lose the religion, even if individuals miss parts of it.
- Religious victory is not sought through forbidden means.
- Some rulings are devotional in character, and surrender to them is part of faith.
