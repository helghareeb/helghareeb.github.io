This notebook gathers selected tafsir and lexical benefits highlighted in the lessons.

## Main lines

- Praise and gratitude overlap, but they are not identical in scope or mode.
- The meaning of a term like *uzlifat* is fixed by context, not by dictionary isolation.
- When *wisdom* is paired with *the Book*, it points to the Prophetic Sunnah.
- The repeated command form with the Messenger indicates the independent authority of obeying him.
- Terms such as *ihsan*, *kalaalah*, and *qur'* require careful contextual reading in law and tafsir.
- Linguistic distinctions, even apparently small ones, shape legal interpretation.
- Context remains decisive in determining whether words refer to people, places, states, or categories.
