This short companion notebook summarizes the reading landmarks that help a student approach Tafsir al-Tabari with the right expectations and tools.

## Main lines

- The right entrance into Tafsir al-Tabari is to understand his method before trying to collect his individual conclusions.
- Much reported disagreement among the salaf becomes clearer once the actual angle of disagreement is identified.
- Al-Tabari's tafsir is a composite science that binds language, transmission, context, fiqh, and disciplined reasoning together.
- The conclusion alone is not enough when reading a major verifying imam; the path to the conclusion matters.
- One of the finest fruits of this study path is that it trains the reader how to read, not only what to memorize.
